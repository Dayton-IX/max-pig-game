Rules:

1. Click Roll dice to roll a pair of dice

2. Whatever the dice land on will be added to your round score (Big Number)

3. Click hold to add your round score to your current score and end your turn

4. If you roll a 1, all of your round score will be deleted and your turn will end

5. If you roll 2 6's in a row all of your current score will be deleted and your turn will end

6. The winner will be the first player who's current score reaches the score to win (Which can be changed to any positive value before the first round, default is 100)

7. Have Fun!
